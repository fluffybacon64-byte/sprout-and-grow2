Our Story

Founded in 2024, Sprout & Grow began as a humble backyard project with a simple goal: to make gardening accessible, sustainable, and joyful for everyone. We believe that plants have the power to transform spaces and elevate our well-being.

Over the years, we have grown into a passionate community of horticulturists, designers, and educators. We specialize in robust, container-friendly plant varieties and educational workshops designed to help your garden thrive, no matter how much space you have.

Our Mission

To cultivate green spaces that bring people together, foster environmental stewardship, and inspire a lifelong love for nature.
